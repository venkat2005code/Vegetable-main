Our Offerings

Fermentation Services

We extend our fermentation expertise beyond retail jars. From supplying top local bistro tables to running custom wedding pickle bars and consulting for regional vegetable farms, we have a service for every size.

Service 01

Wholesale & Restaurant Supply

Equip your restaurant pantry or grocer shelves with our seasonal ferments. We supply restaurants in 5-gallon food-safe tubs and private-labeled retail jars.

  • Custom acidity matching for chef menus
  • Weekly local cooling truck deliveries
  • Volume-based discount models
Wholesale Inquiry
Service 02

Event Catering & Pickle Bars

Bring a unique culinary feature to your wedding, corporate dinner, or private party. We set up beautiful rustic self-serve pickle tasting flights and bar boards.

  • Custom jar tagging for party favors
  • Trained fermentologists on-site for customer Q&As
  • Curated pairings of cheeses, breads, and ferments
Reserve Catering
Service 03

Farm Excess Preservation

Are you a local farm grower struggling with end-of-season crop surplus? We work with farms to ferment, pickle, and jar their excess harvests, turning waste into high-margin shelf products.

  • Lacto-ferment formulation consulting
  • Custom co-packing and label designs
  • Commercial food safety compliance testing
Consultation Form
Service 04

Private Cooking Seminars

Book our kitchen studio for private group masterclasses. Ideal for culinary academies, corporate team-building events, and private cooking clubs.

  • Fully hands-on vegetable prepping stations
  • Brining math and acidity analysis instruction
  • Gourmet cheese and wine pairing lunch included

Corporate Gifting Curation

Send custom-crafted Brine & Barrel crates to partners, employees, and clients. We provide custom wood engraving and curated recipe notebooks matching client food styles.
