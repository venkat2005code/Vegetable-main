Our Heritage

Small Kitchen. Grand Microbes.

Founded in 2021 in a small home pantry, Brine & Barrel Co. was born from a passion for age-old wild fermentation traditions. What started as simple mason jars of cucumbers on kitchen counters has grown into a community fermentary dedicated to clean, raw foods.

We never pasteurize our products, never use artificial preservatives, and source only organic produce. We let the natural bacteria do the work, preserving nutrients and producing superior complex tanginess.

The Standard

The 5 Core Steps to Perfect Brine

How we maintain product excellence across every batch we submerge.

01

Farm Sourcing Inspection

Vegetables are picked and brought to the kitchen within 24 hours. We test texture snap and sugar concentration to ensure organic quality.

02

Manual Slicing & Salting

Produce is hand-prepped, keeping fibers intact. Clean sea salt is massaged in to trigger natural moisture extraction.

03

Submergence & Air-locking

Crated into glass and wood fermenters, fully covered in brine. Custom gas airlocks allow CO2 escape while blocking oxygen.

04

Acidity Curve Monitoring

We trace temperature and pH daily. Only when acidity levels drop below 4.0 pH do we authorize product bottling.

05

Cold Storage Bottling

Packaged raw in amber-tinted jars to block light, then stored at 38°F to decelerate fermentation activity, keeping cultures alive.
